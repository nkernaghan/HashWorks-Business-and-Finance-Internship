Business & Finance Internship

Hybrid — Westport, CT 8 – 12 Weeks Reports to CFO
About the Role

HashWorks Industries is a fast-moving, entrepreneurial company undergoing a period of rapid growth and restructuring.

The candidate will primarily work directly with the CFO and one of the Founding Partners (COO) on finance and operational projects to build out the infrastructure and financial systems necessary to support the company's growth.

The candidate will also gain exposure to other members of the senior management team across multiple divisions of the business.

What This Role Offers
  • Real responsibility and meaningful exposure to senior leadership in a dynamic, fast-moving, and growth-oriented company.
  • Direct access to and mentorship from a Founding Partner (COO) and CFO.
  • Real ownership of projects.
  • Experience how finance, operations, and strategy intersect in a growing organization — with an active role in the outcomes.
  • Practical experience you won't get from a classroom or a large-company internship.
Who We're Looking For
The Ideal Candidate
  • Pursuing a degree in Finance, Accounting, Business, or a related field.
  • Organized, analytical, and detail-oriented — but also flexible and pragmatic.
  • Comfortable working independently and in small teams in a fast-moving, entrepreneurial environment.
  • Comfortable taking initiative while independently solving problems.
Interests That Align
  • Corporate-level investment and deal structuring.
  • Strategic decision-making at the management level of a fast-growing organization.
  • Building out finance functions — from systems and controls to budgeting and investments.
What You'll Work On
Core Projects
01

General Ledger Implementation

Support the implementation of a general ledger platform, working through process design, data organization, testing, and system documentation.

02

Operating Budgets & P&Ls

Create operating budget templates, P&Ls, and cost-allocation methodologies across business units to enable planning, accountability, and variance tracking.

03

Internal Controls

Assist in developing and formalizing internal controls that scale with the business.

04

Travel & Expense Process

Develop a T&E process from policy creation to workflow design, replacing existing practices.

05

Multi-Family Office Investments

Support analyses related to multi-family office investments, including financial models, investment memos, performance tracking, and due diligence materials.

Bonus Opportunity

Candidates could also have the opportunity to work on financial modeling and valuation for a multi-billion-dollar, multi-gigawatt data center campus and digital asset business.
